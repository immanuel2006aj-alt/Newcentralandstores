FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Need Help?

Why are only 130 products shown?

Central Stores offers more than 15,000+ grocery, household and daily essential products. Our website currently highlights the 130 most commonly purchased products for faster browsing and ordering.

We are continuously updating our catalogue and will be adding thousands of additional products in future updates.

Do you provide Wholesale and Retail orders?

Yes. We serve both Retail and Wholesale customers.

Wholesale Orders
Minimum order value: ₹2,000. Delivery is available based on our delivery terms.

Retail Orders
Place your order online or by phone. We will pack your order, and you can conveniently collect it from our store.

Which locations do you deliver?

We provide delivery throughout the Mangalam area.

Orders meeting the required minimum purchase value qualify for FREE delivery with no petrol or service charges.

For locations outside our regular delivery zone, please contact our store team for availability.
